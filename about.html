About MaPTech

Map Technologies Company Ltd is a Dar es Salaam–based ICT solutions provider specializing in security, networking, IT infrastructure, digital transformation, branding, and system integration. We deliver innovative, reliable, and secure technology solutions designed to meet the unique needs of businesses and individuals across Tanzania.
